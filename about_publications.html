Based on the Dutch 'Speelfilm Encyclopedie' I created a system to overanalyse the text. The text featured are all from EYE Magzine. I took the 4 columns of the original book and used these for my analysation. The first column is the original text, the second is every word in alphabetical order, the third every syllable in alphabetical order and the last one is every letter in order.
