CABINET OF CONTENT - 2019-2020

3500x2000 mm
WORK IN PROGRESS!

We were tasked to work in groups of three with these display cabinets. We decided to make our own ateliers in these cabinets, because we felt the need to have our own space in the atelier where all the other students of our major are. We worked in these cabinets for a week and a half, and it became like a performance. Everyone could see us work and do our thing, because we were surrounded by these glass walls. But we still fell that this little place gave us the room to express ourselves. The actual exhibition was on the 17th december of 2019.
